Our Story

A journey of passion, craftsmanship, and timeless style

Our Beginnings

My Mom's Clothing Shop was born from a simple dream in 2010 - to create beautiful, handmade clothing that celebrates traditional craftsmanship while embracing modern aesthetics. What started as a small sewing machine in a Tirana apartment has grown into a beloved boutique known for its unique style and quality.

The early days were filled with challenges, but also with the joy of seeing our first customers fall in love with our creations. Each piece was (and still is) made with meticulous attention to detail, using high-quality fabrics and traditional techniques passed down through generations.

As word spread about our distinctive designs and commitment to quality, we gradually expanded, moving to our first physical storefront in 2015. Today, we're proud to serve customers who appreciate the artistry behind handmade fashion.

Meet The Founder

Elena

Designer & Creative Director

With over 25 years of experience in fashion design, Elena brings a wealth of knowledge and creativity to every collection. Her journey began as a young girl learning to sew from her grandmother, developing a deep appreciation for traditional Albanian textiles and techniques.

After studying fashion design in Italy, Elena returned to Tirana with a vision to blend contemporary styles with Albania's rich sartorial heritage. Her designs reflect this unique fusion - modern silhouettes adorned with intricate embroidery and traditional motifs.

"For me, clothing is more than fabric and thread - it's a way to carry forward our cultural identity while embracing the future," Elena says. This philosophy continues to guide every piece created at My Mom's Clothing Shop.

Our Journey

2010

Founded in a small apartment with just a sewing machine and big dreams. First collection launched with 10 unique designs.

2012

First fashion show held in Tirana, gaining local recognition for innovative designs blending traditional and modern elements.

2015

Opened our first boutique in the heart of Tirana, marking a major milestone in our growth.

2018

Launched our e-commerce platform, making our collections accessible worldwide while maintaining artisanal quality.

2023

Celebrated our 13th anniversary with a special collection honoring Albania's textile heritage, featured in Vogue Italia.
